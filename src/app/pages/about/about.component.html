nCoV19 SG Dashboard

This is a dashboard which provides the latest situation of the 2019 Novel Coronavirus (COVID-19) in Singapore.

Data is maintained by the John Hopkins University Center for Systems Science and Engineering (JHU CCSE) and exposed via an API from @ExpDev07

Give a shoutout of appreciation (#ncov19fighton, #ncov19fightonsg) to encourage:

  • Frontline workers working round the clock to fight against the epidemic
  • Researchers working on a vaccine
  • Data analysts maintaining accurate operational data
